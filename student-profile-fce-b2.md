# Student Profile & Course Setup

*Reference spec for creating test materials for this student. Attach this file at the start of future sessions so it can be reused directly.*

## Level

- Current: B1+ to B2, working towards FCE/B2
- Vocabulary development is the major focus
- Grammar: challenging but accessible at B2
- Needs repeated recycling of vocabulary across different task types

## Test Philosophy

- Priority split: Vocabulary 60–70% / Grammar 30–40%
- Difficulty: B2, context-based, more challenging than standard textbook exercises
- Tasks should require understanding of meaning, usage, collocation, and nuance — not just recognition
- Structure follows Cambridge FCE exam formats where possible, for exam-technique transfer

## Timing Options

- 15-minute quiz
- 25-minute vocabulary-heavy test
- 30-minute progress test
- 45–60 minute comprehensive assessment

## Format Bank

**A. Vocabulary** — V1 Multiple Choice Meaning · V2 Multiple Choice Usage · V3 Gap Fill with Word Bank · V4 Matching Words & Definitions · V5 Matching Collocations · V6 Phrasal Verb Matching · V7 Odd-One-Out / Confusing Words · V8 Word Formation (Cambridge Part 3 style) · V9 Vocabulary Error Correction · V10 Synonym Selection · V11 Antonym Selection

**B. Grammar** — G1 Multiple Choice Grammar · G2 Correct Form of the Word · G3 Correct Form in Context · G4 Error Correction · G5 Rewrite Using Given Word · G6 Sentence Transformation · G7 Article Exercise · G8 Preposition Exercise · G9 Verb Pattern (gerund/infinitive) · G10 Passive Transformation · G11 Conditional Transformation (0–3, mixed) · G12 Tense Classification · G13 Tense Justification · G14 Mixed Grammar Cloze

**C. Open Production** — O1 Open Cloze (FCE Part 2) · O2 Controlled Completion · O3 Sentence Completion · O4 Guided Sentence Writing · O5 Guided Paragraph Writing (5 target words)

**D. Cambridge Use of English** — U1 Multiple Choice Cloze (FCE Part 1) · U2 Open Cloze (FCE Part 2) · U3 Word Formation (FCE Part 3) · U4 Key Word Transformation (FCE Part 4)

**E. Reading-Based** — R1 Multiple Choice · R2 Gapped Text (FCE Reading Part 6) · R3 Multiple Matching (FCE Reading Part 7) · R4 True/False/Not Given
